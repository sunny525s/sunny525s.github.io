About Me
Hi! I'm a Junior at Cornell University, studying Computer Science and Operations Research. My interests lie in the intersection of Machine Learning and Math, Computer Vision and Robotics, and Fullstack Development. I specialize in vision-based robotics and aerial systems where I develop algorithms for building intelligent autonomous devices.
On campus, I conduct research with the PoRTaL Research Group under the mentorship of Professor Sanjiban Choudhury where we work on building accessible, user-friendly robots for practical everyday tasks. I'm also part of the Intelligent Systems Subteam at Cornell University Unmanned Air Systems, a student led project team focused on building autonomous aircrafts.
I was formerly a Software Engineer Intern at Merck & Co. where I worked on developing a fullstack dashboard for the High-Performance Computing Team. I also previously worked as a Research Intern at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and Brookhaven National Laboratory on Reinforcement Learning and Fourier and Image Reconstruction Projects.
